about us

Shoomai was born from a deep love for the timeless tradition of dim sum and the communal spirit it represents. Our founders grew up surrounded by the comforting aromas of bustling tea houses, where every meal brought people together. Inspired by these cherished memories, they set out to create a place where authentic craftsmanship meets contemporary creativity.

Therefore, at Shoomai, we bring you the finest selection of traditional dim sum, crafted with authentic recipes and the freshest ingredients. Our chefs are dedicated to preserving the rich culinary heritage of dim sum while infusing it with a modern twist. Whether you're craving classic dumplings, steamed buns, or savory pastries, Shoomai offers a delightful dining experience that captures the essence of this beloved cuisine. Join us for a journey of flavors and textures that celebrate the art of dim sum.
